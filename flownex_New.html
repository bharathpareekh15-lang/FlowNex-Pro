What you get

Everything your business needs.
Nothing you don't.

Built specifically for Indian SMEs, manufacturers, traders, and EPC companies across Pune and beyond.

Drag-and-drop dashboard builder
Build stunning MIS dashboards in minutes. Pick widgets, connect your data source, and your dashboard is live. Zero code, zero IT help needed.
Live ERP data integration
Connect Tally, SAP, Oracle, or your custom ERP in one click. FlowNex understands ERP field structures — no manual mapping required.
Smart risk alerts
Get automatic WhatsApp and email alerts when stock dips below threshold, payments are overdue, or production targets are at risk.
Automated MIS reports
Schedule daily, weekly, or monthly MIS reports to be auto-generated and emailed or WhatsApp'd to your management team. No manual effort.
Multi-language support
Full support for English, Hindi, Marathi, Kannada, and more. Your factory floor manager reads in Marathi, your CFO reads in English — same data.
Role-based access control
Admins see everything. Managers see their department. Field users see only their tasks. Fine-grained permissions that match your org chart.
